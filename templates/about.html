About Our Trading Platform

Our Real-Time Stock Trading Platform is an educational web application designed to help users understand how stock markets work in a risk-free environment.

This platform allows users to practice buying and selling stocks using virtual money while experiencing real market behavior through historical and simulated real-time data.

Our Objective

  • Provide hands-on experience in stock trading
  • Help beginners understand market trends
  • Allow safe learning without real financial risk

Technologies Used

  • Python & Flask (Backend)
  • HTML, CSS & JavaScript (Frontend)
  • Pandas for market data handling

⚠️ This platform is strictly for educational purposes. No real money transactions are involved.
